Quarto Board Game



Quatro Board Game Rules

Quarto is a board game for two players invented by Swiss mathematician Blaise Müller.The game
is played on a 4×4 board.There are 16 unique pieces to play with, each of which is either:

  • tall or short;
  • light or dark;
  • square or circular;
  • and hollow-top or solid-top.


    • Players take turns choosing a piece which the other player must then place on the board. A player
    wins by placing a piece on the board which forms a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row of four
    pieces, all of which have a common attribute (all short, all circular, etc.).
